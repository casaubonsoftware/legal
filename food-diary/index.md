# Privacy Policy — How I Ate

**Last updated: 23 July 2026**

This Privacy Policy explains how the **How I Ate** mobile app ("the App", "we",
"us") collects, uses, and protects your information. How I Ate lets you log meals
by photo and record digestive symptoms to help you spot patterns over time.

The App is provided by **Casaubon B.V.**, based in **the Netherlands**. Your use of
the App is also governed by our [Terms of Use](terms.html). If you have any questions,
contact us at **support@casaubonsoftware.nl**.

---

## 1. Information we collect

We only collect what the App needs to work. You do **not** have to give us your name
to use How I Ate — you can use it with an anonymous account.

| Data | What it is | Why we collect it |
|------|-----------|-------------------|
| **Meal photos** | Photos you take or select to log a meal | To store your meal and to identify its food components using an AI vision service (see §3) |
| **Food & allergen data** | The list of components in a meal and their allergen/intolerance groups (e.g. gluten, dairy) | To build your diary and power the Insights correlations |
| **Symptom logs (health data)** | The body areas you tap, a severity rating (1–10), an optional note, and the time | To record symptoms and correlate them with meals |
| **Account email & password** | *Optional* — only if you choose to "secure your account" | So your diary survives a reinstall or new phone, and so you can sign in again |
| **Device identifier** | A reinstall-stable identifier generated on your device | To fairly enforce the free monthly limit on AI photo analyses and prevent abuse |
| **AI usage events** | A timestamped record that an analysis happened | To count usage against your plan's quota |
| **Subscription status** | Whether you have an active "Premium" subscription | To unlock paid features; purchases are handled by the app stores and RevenueCat (see §3) |

We do **not** collect your precise location, contacts, or advertising identifiers,
and the App does **not** contain third-party advertising.

## 2. How we use your information

- To provide the core features: logging meals and symptoms, and showing Insights.
- To identify food components in your meal photos via an AI vision service.
- To enforce plan limits (free vs. Premium) fairly.
- To operate subscriptions and restore purchases.
- To keep the service secure and prevent abuse.

We do **not** sell your personal data, and we do **not** use it for advertising.

## 3. Third parties who process your data

We use a small number of trusted service providers ("processors") to run the App.
Data is shared with them only to provide the service:

- **Supabase** — hosts our database, authentication, and the private storage bucket
  where your meal photos are kept. Your data is protected by row-level security so
  one account cannot read another's data.
- **Together AI** — when you ask the App to analyse a meal photo, that photo is sent
  to **Together AI** (together.ai), based in the United States, purely to return a
  list of food components.
- **RevenueCat** — manages subscription status and validates purchases.
- **Google Play / Apple App Store** — process subscription payments. We never
  receive your full payment card details.

## 4. Data retention

- Your meals, symptoms, and account data are kept until **you delete them or delete
  your account**.
- AI usage records are automatically deleted after **60 days**.
- Anonymous accounts that contain **no** meals or symptoms are automatically removed
  after **14 days** of inactivity. An account that contains any data is never
  auto-deleted.

## 5. Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time from
inside the App:

**Open the App → Account → Delete account.**

This removes your meals, meal photos, symptom logs, subscription record, and usage
history, and cannot be undone. If you are unable to use the in-app option, email us
at **support@casaubonsoftware.nl** and we will delete your data on request.

## 6. Security

Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). Meal photos are stored in a private bucket
accessible only to your account. Access to the underlying database is restricted by
row-level security policies. No online service can be guaranteed 100% secure, but we
take reasonable measures to protect your information.

## 7. Children's privacy

How I Ate is not directed to children and is intended for users aged 16 and over
(or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from
children. If you believe a child has provided us data, contact us and we will delete
it.

## 8. Your rights

Depending on where you live (for example under the EU/UK GDPR), you may have the
right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal data, and to withdraw
consent. You can exercise most of these directly in the App, or by contacting us at
**support@casaubonsoftware.nl**.

## 9. Medical disclaimer

How I Ate is a self-tracking tool, **not** a medical device, and does not provide
medical advice or diagnosis. The Insights it shows are informational correlations
only. Always consult a qualified healthcare professional about symptoms or dietary
decisions.
